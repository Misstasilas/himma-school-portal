School History

HIMMA NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL was established to provide quality Islamic and western education for children in Jange Yari-Bori, Kafur Local Government Area of Katsina State, Nigeria. The school focuses on academic excellence, moral discipline, leadership training and community development. Over the years, the school has continued to nurture young learners into responsible future leaders.
