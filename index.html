School History

HIMMA NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL was established to provide quality education for children in Jange Yari-Bori, Kafur Local Government Area, Katsina State, Nigeria. The school combines western and Islamic education to nurture responsible future leaders with strong academic and moral values.
